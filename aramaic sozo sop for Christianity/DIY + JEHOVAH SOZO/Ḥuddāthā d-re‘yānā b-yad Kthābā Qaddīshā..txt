Dḥoq menākh maḥshabāthā bīshāthā, aw maḥshabāthā d-ga’wā, aykh: “Ant hū krisṭyānā ṭāb men kol ‘ālmā.”
Reject negative thoughts or prideful thoughts, like: “You are the world’s best Christian.”

Ezil bātar madbrānūthā d-qeryānā d-Kthābā Qaddīshā.
Follow the Bible reading guide.

En īth lakh mushklā, aykh deḥlā, qrī w-emar b-qālā rāmā pethgāmē d-Kthābā Qaddīshā ‘al deḥlā, d-metsē at d-teshkaḥ b-Google.
If you have a problem like fear, read and speak out loud Bible scriptures on fear that you can Google search.

Hālēn maḥshabāthā bīshāthā hennēn medem d-Sāṭānā sābar ‘laykh, w-hennēn kadhābē.
These bad thoughts are what Satan thinks of you, and they are lies.

Zkī l-dukthā d-nīḥūthā dīlākh.
Overcome your comfort zone.

Tuphsā:
Example:

En dāḥel at men barā, aykh dukyāthā d-rabbē ‘ammā, b-zabnā w-qalīl qalīl, nasseh d-tepuq yattīr w-yattīr l-dukyāthā d-rabbē ‘ammā, w-eda‘ d-ant shappīr at.
If you are afraid of outside, like public places, slowly over time, little by little, try to go out more and more to public places, and realise you are okay.
