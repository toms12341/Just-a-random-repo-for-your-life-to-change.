Qri kol medem, lā teqreh palgā.
Read it all, do not half read this.

Sāneq at d-tṣalleh ‘al lawṭā. Hādeh medem mparash hū, w-Alāhā lā ṣāveh d-tlawet bnaynāshā.
You must pray about cursing. It is very specific, and God does not want you to curse people.

Ethdakkar: brikh at kad metlawet at, w-ṣalleh kad metradaph at. Hādeh qānōnā qadmayā hū.
Remember: blessed when cursed, and pray when persecuted. This is the number one rule.

Metse at d-tlawet, lākin b-medem mparash balḥud. Lā akh ḥarshuthā, elā emar: hādeh b-ṣebyānēh d-Alāhā.
You can curse, but only for specific things. Not like witchcraft, but saying it is God’s choice.

Shma‘ hākhā.
Listen here.

En qnēh d-nūrā samīm ‘laykh, lawet qnēh d-nūrā, w-net‘abed taḥt raḥmē.
If a gun is pointed at you, curse the gun, and may it work under mercy.

En sakinā samīm ‘laykh, lawet sakinā, w-net‘abed taḥt raḥmē.
If a knife is pointed at you, curse the knife, and may it work under mercy.

Hudā‘ā:
Disclaimer:

Lā teshreh hādeh b-dukhyāthā d-ḥubbā, aw b-dukhyāthā d-lā ith lakh hudāyā men Ruḥā d-Qudshā, aw d-lā margesh at b-qaṭlā aw sakkanā.
Do not release this in loving situations, or places where you are not guided by the Holy Spirit, or where you do not feel danger.

Metse at d-teshreh pelguthā ‘al ba‘el-dbabē dīlākh, d-lā neqrebun lakh.
You can release division against your enemies so they do not come near you.

Alāhā sāneh pelguthā, lākin kad sāneq hū — akh men “at qadmayā” l-“at ḥadthā” — Alāhā ṣāveh b-hādeh.
God hates division, but when needed — like from the old you to the new you — God approves it.

Mparash hākhannā: neturā balḥud.
Specifically like this: protection only.

Rāḥem anā lawṭā kad īth sakkanā b-sakinā, lākin lā kad bnāshā ṣāḥē ‘lay b-lawṭā. Sakinā neppel, w-at tetnattar taḥt neturā.
I accept cursing when there is danger with a knife, but not when someone yells a curse at me. The knife may fall, and you may be protected under protection.

Lawet mānā, akh qnēh d-nūrā, kad at b-sakkanā.
Curse the item, like a gun, when you are in danger.

Shri pelguthā ‘al ba‘el-dbabē dīlākh, en ātēn ‘laykh — neturā balḥud — w-sabar l-ṣlothā d-qarbā ruḥānāyā.
Release division against your enemies if they are coming for you — protection only — and wait for warfare prayer.

Lawṭē l-‘elāthā kēnathā d-īth b-kthābā qaddīshā, w-d-hen ṣebyānēh d-Alāhā, metse Alāhā d-negbeh lawṭā aw lawṭē.
Curses for righteous causes that are biblically sound, and are God’s choice, can align as God can choose the curse or curses.
