Hawē tāwē ‘al ḥṭāhaykh.
Be sorry for your sins.

Shbīqīn lakh, ellā Alāhā sāneh ḥṭīthā.
You are forgiven, but God hates sin.

Alāhā hū Alāhā ṭannānā.
God is a jealous God.

D-gābeh ḥṭīthā, gābeh Sāṭānā; w-besrā ḥalāsh hū l-ḥṭīthā.
Choosing sin is choosing Satan, and the flesh is weak to sin.

Īth ṭaybūthā, w-sagyē ḥṭāhē metkasēn, ellā snī ḥṭīthā.
There is grace, and many sins are covered, but hate sin.

Lā teṣa‘er l-Ruḥā d-ṭaybūthā.
Do not insult the Spirit of grace.

Bekhī lah men lebbākh. Tūb, w-eda‘ man rāḥem lakh.
Cry it out from your heart. Repent, and realise who loves you.

Yeshua. Lā Sāṭānā.
Jesus. Not Satan.
