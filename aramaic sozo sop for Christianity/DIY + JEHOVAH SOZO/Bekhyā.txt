En lā metse at d-tebkeh, sha’el men Alāhā d-na‘bed lakh d-tebkeh.

You are not weak for crying.
Lā hawet ḥalāsh metul d-bekhet.

Sometimes it is healthy.
Zabnīn īth d-hādeh ḥlīmā hī.

Sometimes you just need to become stronger.
W-zabnīn īth d-sāneq at d-teḥsan yattīr.

Give yourself permission to cry so you heal quicker.
Hab l-nafshākh rukhṣā d-tebkeh, d-tet’aseh b-‘gal.

Also, it is a good sign that God loves you.
W-tūb, hādeh āthā ṭābtā hī d-Alāhā rāḥem lakh.

Eat a good meal. Do something you like after, and praise God.
Akhul me’kultā ṭābtā. W-bātar hākhannā ‘bed medem d-raḥem at, w-shabbaḥ l-Alāhā.
