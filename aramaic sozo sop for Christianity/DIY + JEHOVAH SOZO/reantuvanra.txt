Yeshua, pruqli w-‘addar lī d-etūb men ḥṭāhay.
Jesus, save me and help me repent of my sins.

Metul ḥṭīthā, bar-nāsh metmaḥē rūḥānāyā yattīr qashyā.
Because of sin, a person gets spiritually beaten harder.

Yeshua shqal ṣlībā, d-lā tēzal l-gehannā.
Jesus took the cross so you would not go to hell.

Hab lī Ruḥā d-Qudshā. Tawdi, Abbā.
Give me the Holy Spirit. Thank you, Father.

Wālē d-tet‘amad b-kulleh šūqā‘ā — kul lakh tetṭabba‘ b-mayyā.
You must be fully submerged to be baptised.

‘Amed nafshākh b-shmeh d-Abbā, w-d-Brā, w-d-Ruḥā d-Qudshā. Mattay 28:19.
Baptise yourself in the name of the Father, and the Son, and the Holy Spirit. Matthew 28:19.

En at baḥūdāykh, w-layt man d-na‘med lakh, ‘bed aykh Mattay 28:19 b-yammā, aw b-birkā, aw b-masḥyānā, w-shwar b-mayyā b-kulleh šūqā‘ā.
If you are alone and no one can baptise you, do Matthew 28:19 at the beach, pool, or bath, and jump in fully submerged.

Qatoliqē metparqīn b-haymānuthā — aykh ḥalē d-kol bar-nāsh — ellā īth lhon mawhebtē daggālē. Ṣalli ‘al hādeh.
Catholics are saved by faith, depending on individual circumstances, but they have false gifts. Pray about it.

Qabbel medem ṭābā: Ruḥā d-Qudshā sharīrā, w-ma‘mūdithā d-mayyā.
Get the good stuff: the real Holy Spirit and water baptism.

‘Edtā hī ‘ammā, lā binyānā.
Church is the people, not the building.

Lā ḥayyāb at d-tezal l-‘edtā d-binyānā; ellā hawē ‘am baytākh aykh ‘edtā, en lā ṣābe at d-tezal l-binyānā.
You do not have to go to the church building; be with your family as church if you do not feel like going to the building.

Aw ‘am raḥmē d-raḥem at. Nasseh d-tehweh ‘am Mshīḥāyē.
Or with friends you like. Try to spend time with Christians.

Hū mīth mawtā kīwānā w-qashyā, ḥlāf yārthūthākh w-ḥayāykh. Ṣalli ‘al hādeh.
He died a painful death in exchange for your inheritance and your life. Pray about it.
