Lā yāda‘ at mā ganab, aw qṭal, aw ḥarreb.
You do not know what he has stolen, killed, or destroyed.

Megnab, qāṭel, w-mḥarreb = Sāṭānā, b‘el-dbebā d-barnāshūthā. Yoḥannān 10:10.
Steal, kill, and destroy = Satan, the enemy of humanity. John 10:10.

Aykh dēmā:
For example:

En īth lakh le’ūthā ammīnā, hū ganab ḥaylākh. Qṭal rukhākh. Ḥarreb ḥadwāthākh bātar b-yawmā.
If you have chronic fatigue, he has stolen your energy, killed your mood, and destroyed your happiness later in the day.

En lā metse at d-tetkattal, qrī l-raḥmē, w-sha’el snāyā kenā d-ḥṭīthā.
If you cannot fight, call for mercy and ask for righteous hatred of sin.
